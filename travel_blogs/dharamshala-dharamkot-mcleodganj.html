Dharamshala, Dharamkot, McLeodganj

Himachal Pradesh | peaceful hills and Tibetan calm

Himalayan Quiet

Dharamshala was a slow, soulful pause in the mountains. Sacred monasteries, winding streets, and tea gardens made every day feel both simple and rich. It was the kind of journey where sunrise over prayer flags felt more meaningful than any itinerary, and where the city’s quiet energy left a deep, lasting calm.
