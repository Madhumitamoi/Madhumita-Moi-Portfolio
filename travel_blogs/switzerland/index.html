Switzerland

Switzerland felt like a breath of fresh air: crisp alpine mornings, still lakes reflecting sharp mountain peaks, and quaint villages wrapped in mist. Every path led to another postcard-perfect view, and each relaxed moment was balanced by the thrill of reaching a higher ridge. These photos are the quiet lake scenes, the big sky mountain days, and the soft glow of rural Switzerland.
