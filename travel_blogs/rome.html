Rome

Rome is where history comes alive - ancient ruins that whisper stories of emperors and gladiators, piazzas that buzz with modern Italian life, and gelato that tastes like liquid sunshine. From throwing coins into the Trevi Fountain to wandering the Colosseum at sunset, every corner holds a piece of eternity. The city's chaotic energy, the warmth of its people, and the perfect blend of ancient and modern created memories that feel as timeless as the city itself.
